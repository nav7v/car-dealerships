About the Manufacturer

The Mercedes-AMG GT is a series of high-performance sports cars manufactured by Mercedes-AMG, a subsidiary of Mercedes-Benz. It is produced at the Mercedes-Benz plant in Sindelfingen, Germany. The first-generation AMG GT was introduced in 2014 and has seen various variants, including the GT3 and GT4 racing models, as well as high-performance versions like the GT R and the Black Series. The second-generation AMG GT, based on the same platform as the new SL, is also available and includes a plug-in hybrid version.
